Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

ClinicFloww is a local-first clinic management software. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information when you use our software and services.

2. Data Storage & Privacy

Our application operates on a fully local basis. All patient records, clinic data, and operational information are stored exclusively on your local devices or your self-managed infrastructure. We do not transmit, sync, or store this data on any external servers controlled by ClinicFloww.

3. Google OAuth Usage

To facilitate secure login, ClinicFloww uses Google Workspace APIs via Google OAuth. Our use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • We only request necessary scopes (email and profile) for authentication purposes.
  • We do not store, process, or transmit your authentication tokens to any external servers. All authentication handling remains on your local deployment.

4. Data Sharing & Selling

Because we do not have access to your locally stored data, we do not sell, share, or rent any data to third parties.

5. Your Responsibilities

As the operator of a local-first deployment, you are solely responsible for ensuring the physical and digital security of the devices hosting the software, maintaining backups, and complying with any local healthcare data regulations applicable to your practice.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@clinicfloww.com.
